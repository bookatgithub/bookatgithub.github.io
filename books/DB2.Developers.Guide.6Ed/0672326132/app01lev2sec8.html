The Sample STOGROUP

The storage group used by the sample database is DSN8G410. The following statement is provided by IBM to define the sample STOGROUP. (Of course, the VOLUMES, VCAT, andPASSWORD information are usually modified prior to the creation of the storage group.)







CREATE STOGROUP DSN8G810

  VOLUMES (DSNV01)

  VCAT     DSNC810;;
