Chapter 38. DB2 Contingency Planning

IN THIS CHAPTER

  • What Is a Disaster?

  • DB2 Environmental Considerations

  • DB2 Contingency Planning Guidelines

Contingency planning for disaster recovery is a complex task in the best of situations. Unfortunately, the best of situations does not exist in a DB2 environment. This chapter defines the limitations of DB2 in the framework of disaster recovery and suggests solutions to the problems that these limitations create. This chapter pertains to the recovery of DB2 application data, not to the recovery of the DB2 subsystem (and related data).

Suggestions, cautions, requirements, and techniques are provided to help you create a disaster recovery plan for your DB2 applications.
