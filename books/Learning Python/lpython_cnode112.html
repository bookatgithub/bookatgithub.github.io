Part III: Appendixes

This last part of the book consists of three appendixes that are mostly pointers to other sources of information.

  • Appendix A, presents general Python resources such as sources of documation, advice, and software.

  • Appendix B, covers resources that are specific to certain operating systems.

  • Appendix C, lists the answers to all the exercises presented at the end of chapters in Part I and Part II.
