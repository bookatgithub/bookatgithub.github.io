A.7 Tim Peters

He's not paid to do it, but a fellow by the name of Tim Peters is known to answer more questions about Python on the newsgroup than anyone in their right mind should. His comments are not only wise and helpful but quite often hilarious as well. Rumor has it that he is a living human being, although we have never met him in person, so there's always the possibility that he's just a nicely crafted Python program. Come to think of it, only one person we have talked to claims to have met Tim in person, and that's Guido. Makes you wonder...
